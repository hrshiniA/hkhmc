About us

Established in 2001, Hong Kong Healthcare Medical Centre (HKHMC) started first GP clinic in Sai Kung and has since expanded to five locations in Tai Wai, Tsuen Wan, To Kwa Wan, and Wan Chai. HKHMC remains committed to delivering high-quality, affordable healthcare services that exceed patient expectations

Our Services

HKHMC offers a comprehensive range of quality medical services -
General Practice, Dental Care, and Physical Examination to private
